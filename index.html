LMS Project - Simple Lie algebras in positive characteristic: Summer 2025

I recieved funding from the London Mathematical Society Undergraduate Reserach Bursary to study the Jacobson Witt algebras with Dr Lewis Topley. Our aim was to study the centre of the Universal Enveloping Algebra of the Jacobson Witt algebras, with an end goal of being able to describe the entire centre. As part of the project, I developed a program using the computer algebra package SAGE with Python to work with elements of the Jacobson Witt algebras and elements in the UEA. I am continuing this project for my Masters' Thesis.
