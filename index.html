Backstage Secretary: 2024 - 2025

In the 2024-2025 academic year I took up the role of Secretary on the Backstage Technical Services committee. My primary focus in taking the role was to modernise the way the society was viewed, as well as to advertise the society more widely in order to increase membership numbers and diversity the skillset and interests among the society membership.
One key focus was in upgrading the society's stationary and branding, extending to our logos, documentation, website, and business cards. I developed simple elegant branding guidelines including a clean colour scheme and modernised logo images. Another key focus of my tenure as Secretary was to focus on ways I could help facilitate other committee members, such that they can more easily achieve progress while also balancing their degrees.

Freshers' Week 2023 [Head of Sound]

For FW23 in September 2023, I was tasked with designing and deploying a PA system suitable for an audience of approx. 3000 punters in the University of Bath Founders' Hall for the period of one week. The focus of the design was a D&B Q-Series line-array system comprised of 6 boxes of Q1 either side of the stage, E6 front-fill, and J-Series subwoofers, driven by a stack of D80 & D20 amplifiers. This equipment was provided by Amber Audio. Design for the project began in Summer 2024, with the rig taking place over the course of 2 days and get-out taking place in an overnight push, followed by a short clean up period the following day.
