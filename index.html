Personal Projects

LMS Project - Simple Lie algebras in positive characteristic: Summer 2025

I was awarded the London Mathematical Society Undergraduate Reserach Bursary to study the Jacobson Witt algebras with Dr Lewis Topley. Our aim was to study the central elements, with an end goal of being able to describe generators of the centre of the Universal Enveloping Algebra of a Jacobson Witt algebra in arbitrary rank and characteristic.
As part of the project, I developed a program using the computer algebra package SAGE with Python to work with elements of the Jacobson Witt algebras and elements in the UEA.
Reading for this project included sections from Modular Lie Algebras and their Representations by Strade & Farnsteiner.
